Meet Arioso Quartet
Igor Szwec
Violinist
Mr. Szwec graduated from the Philadelphia Musical Academy, studying with Karen Tuttle. He is currently Concertmaster of the Philly Pops, New Jersey Opera, Savoy Company and AVA Opera, as well as Assistant Concertmaster of the Opera Company of Philadelphia. Mr. Szwec is also a member of the Concerto Soloists Chamber Orchestra, with many solo and chamber music performances.
